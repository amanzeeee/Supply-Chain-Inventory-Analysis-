#  Supply Chain & Inventory Analysis Dashboard

##  Project Overview

This project presents an end-to-end Supply Chain & Inventory Analysis solution using **Excel, MySQL, and Power BI**. It focuses on analyzing warehouse operations, inventory levels, supplier performance, and business KPIs to support data-driven decision-making.

The dashboard provides actionable insights into inventory management, delivery performance, warehouse efficiency, and product revenue through interactive visualizations.

---

##  Business Objectives

- Monitor overall supply chain performance.
- Track inventory levels across warehouses.
- Identify low-stock and overstock products.
- Evaluate supplier delivery performance.
- Analyze revenue trends and product performance.
- Support strategic business decisions using interactive dashboards.

##  Key Insights

- Revenue is primarily generated by a few high-performing products.
- Several products are below the reorder level and require timely replenishment.
- Inventory value is concentrated across selected warehouses.
- Supplier delivery delays impact operational efficiency.
- Monitoring warehouse inventory can reduce stock-out risks.

---

##  Business Recommendations

- Replenish low-stock products proactively.
- Improve supplier delivery performance through SLA monitoring.
- Optimize inventory allocation across warehouses.
- Focus on high-performing product categories.
- Regularly monitor supply chain KPIs for better decision-making.
